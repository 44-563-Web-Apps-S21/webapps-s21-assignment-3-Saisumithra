Wonder Women

Wonder Women is the ideal of girl power and strength. She is faster than mercury and stronger than hercules. She can deflect bullets with her gold bracelets. She has a lasso which forces people, when caught in it, to tell the truth.Wonder women is a positive role model to everyone because she teaches people to do right.She shows that women can flourish in this world and influences girls everyehere to strive for excellence.
